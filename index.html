Platform Features

Everything your team needs.
Nothing they don't.

Built specifically for trades and service businesses — not adapted from generic project management software.

Jobs & Dispatch

Full job lifecycle from first call to final invoice. Assign techs, track status in real time, and dispatch from a drag-and-drop board. Your whole team stays in sync — even in the field.

  • Kanban + list view
  • Real-time status updates
  • GPS tech tracking
  • Customer notifications

Invoicing & Payments

Generate invoices directly from completed jobs. Collect payment in the field or send a payment link. Track what's paid, what's due, and what's overdue.

Inventory Management

Track parts and materials across jobs and trucks. Get low-stock alerts before you're caught short on a job site. Link inventory usage directly to invoices.

Time Cards

Techs clock in and out from their phone. Managers review and approve from the office. Weekly summaries export directly to payroll. No more paper timesheets.

Employee Management

Profiles, certifications, roles, and availability — all in one place. Know who's qualified for what job, who's available today, and who's already booked.

Reporting & Analytics

Know your numbers without a spreadsheet. Revenue by period, tech utilization, job completion rates, top customers, and inventory usage — all updated in real time.

  • Revenue dashboards
  • Tech performance metrics
  • Job completion rates
  • Inventory cost tracking

Customer CRM

Every customer's full history — jobs, invoices, equipment, and notes — in one profile. Call a customer and know everything before you say hello.

Mobile PWA

Works on any phone, no app store required. Techs get their schedule, job details, and can clock in/out right from their browser — online or offline.
